Assay Development Process

Structured, Transparent, and Cost-Effective

Our Services

A complete assay development, with NEW Cos leading the first three stages with flexible support for later stages, either through your in-house team or our internal assay development experts.

Stage 1

Bioreceptor Screening 
(if needed)

Timeline: ~4 weeks
  
Overview: We identify optimal antibody candidates tailored to your specific target, establishing a solid foundation for assay development.

Stage 2

Assay Feasibility & Proof of Concept

Timeline: 4–6 months, with regular collaborative updates
  
Overview: This phase generates critical early data, including Limit of Detection (LOD), dynamic range, and assay duration, confirming the assay’s viability through iterative testing.

Stage 3

Further Development & Early Assay Optimization

Timeline: 3–4 months (Timelines may vary based on project complexity)
  
Overview: We refine the assay to enhance sensitivity, specificity, and reproducibility, optimizing key parameters to align with your performance goals and prepare for integration into your workflow and assay transfer to your team.

Flexible Pathways: Partner with Us or Transition In-House

From Stage 4 onwards, you can continue working with NEW Cos or transition to internal management. Either way, we offer flexible support through our experienced network of regulatory, clinical, and manufacturing partners.

  • ⚫
    Stage 4: Regulatory Strategy

We help define the regulatory pathway for your device, including classification, risk analysis, and pre-submission planning—tailored for your target market(s).

  • ⚫
    Stage 5: Preclinical Testing

We guide or manage preclinical testing to validate device safety, stability, and analytical performance under controlled conditions.

  • ⚫
    Stage 6: Clinical Trial Implementation

We coordinate or support clinical evaluations to demonstrate real-world effectiveness, user performance, and compliance with regulatory requirements.

  • ⚫
    Stage 7: Regulatory Submission

Our team assists in preparing and compiling regulatory submissions (e.g., CE/UKCA, FDA 510(k), IVDR), ensuring documentation is complete, accurate, and audit-ready.

  • ⚫
    Stage 8: Production Setup

We help you establish or scale up manufacturing—either in-house or with trusted partners—ensuring your assay and device are production-ready under GMP/QMS standards.

  • ⚫
    Stage 9: Quality Control

We implement robust quality control protocols to maintain consistency, compliance, and reliability throughout production.

  • ⚫
    Stage 10: ​Commercial Launch

From technical marketing to go-to-market support, we help bring your device to market confidently—with a validated product, supporting materials, and regulatory clearance in hand.
