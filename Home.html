Assay Types

Our new Plasmonicℏip supports a diverse range of assay formats, enabling versatile applications

Chemical Sensing: Analyze chemical compounds for industrial or environmental applications. 

Immunoassays: Detect and quantify proteins or biomarkers for diagnostics and R&D.

Cellular Assays: Study cellular responses with high sensitivity. 

Label-Free, Labelled, or Plasmonic Amplification Assays (Patent-Protected): Maximize sensitivity and flexibility. 

Synthetic Capture-Based Assays: Utilize aptamers, oligonucleotides, or other capture molecules. 

Small Molecule Assays: Ideal for drug testing, toxicology, and metabolite analysis. 

Enzyme Assays: Monitor enzymatic activity with precision.
